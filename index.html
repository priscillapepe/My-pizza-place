Pizza

Is a savory dish of Italianorigin consisting of a usually round,flattened base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes,cheese,and often various other ingredients such as:

  1. Anchovies
  2. Mushrooms
  3. Onions
  4. Olives
  5. Pineapple
  6. meat

It is baked at high temperature,traditionally in a wood-fired oven. Asmall pizza is called Pizzetta.
